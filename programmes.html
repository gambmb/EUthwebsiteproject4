Programmes

Learn locally. Connect across borders.

EU'th combines practical language learning with peer support, youth leadership and European exchange.

A

Free English workshops

Community-based sessions for youth aged 16–25, designed around real-life communication, employment and confidence.

⇄

Cross-border exchanges

Shared projects and mobility experiences connecting young people across Hungary, Slovakia and Romania.

◉

Youth-worker training

Training local educators and youth workers to deliver inclusive English sessions in their own communities.

⌁

Digital resources

Online learning materials in local languages to support independent study and reach remote areas.

How learning happens

Practical, social and youth-led.

Peer-to-peer learning

Study groups and informal practice build confidence without the pressure of a traditional classroom.

Community workshops

Schools and youth centres host accessible sessions close to where participants live.

Digital culture

Social media, online challenges and youth-friendly tools support continuous learning.
