Environmental commitment

Low-impact learning by design.

As an education-based NGO with no manufacturing or physical product supply chain, EU'th begins with a naturally light footprint.

Digital-first delivery

  • Online resources reduce printed materials
  • Digital infrastructure minimises paper and material waste
  • Local venues avoid dedicated premises

Greener mobility

  • Train travel prioritised over flights where possible
  • Shared group transport for exchange programmes
  • Travel planning aligned with EU sustainability goals
Environmental risks

Measured honestly, managed responsibly.

Cross-border travel emissions

Exchange programmes create transport emissions, which the project seeks to reduce through rail-first planning and shared travel.

Digital energy use

Servers and devices have an energy footprint, so resources should be efficient, reusable and hosted responsibly.
