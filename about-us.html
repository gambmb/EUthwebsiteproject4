About us

Built by young people, for young people.

EU'th was created by Guilherme, Greta, Nikita, Boriska and Kaia as a youth-led association with one simple belief: English should open doors, not create barriers.

Our story

Why we created EU'th.

We started EU'th because we saw how much English can affect a young person's confidence, education, work opportunities and ability to connect across borders.

For many young people, especially in rural or lower-income communities, quality English support is still difficult to access. Private lessons can be expensive, local opportunities can be limited and many learners do not get enough safe spaces to practise speaking.

EU'th exists to make that support more equal, more practical and more encouraging. Our goal is not only to teach a language, but to help young people feel that their voice belongs in Europe.

What brought us together

  • A shared belief in equal access to education
  • Personal experience of language learning and international communication
  • A desire to connect young people across Central Europe
  • The belief that confidence grows through practice, friendship and support
  • A commitment to youth-led solutions, not top-down decisions

How we work

An association shaped by trust.

  • We listen to young people before designing activities
  • We work with schools, youth centres and local partners
  • We keep programmes free and accessible wherever possible
  • We encourage peer support instead of judgement
  • We measure impact through confidence, participation and opportunity
Our message

We are not building EU'th for young people from a distance. We are building it with them.

That is why the association is youth-led, community-focused and designed around real barriers that young people face when trying to learn, speak and take part.
