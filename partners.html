Partners & funding

A network built for local trust and European reach.

EU'th depends on strong relationships with education providers, public institutions, funders and youth organisations.

Erasmus+ agencies

National agencies and Key Action 2 cooperation structures across the five target countries.

Schools & youth centres

Local delivery partners that provide trusted spaces, participant outreach and community knowledge.

Universities

English-language departments, student volunteers and training expertise.

Municipalities

Regional coordination, local co-funding and access to public networks.

Language organisations

Potential collaboration with organisations such as the British Council and equivalent providers.

Community contributors

In-kind support from venues, educators, local businesses and civic groups.

Funding model

Blended support for long-term resilience.

  • Erasmus+ Key Action 2 grant as the primary source
  • Municipal and regional co-funding
  • Foundation grants and private donations
  • In-kind contributions from partner organisations
